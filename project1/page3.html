Ranking all of the dining halls on campus

Being that I do attend Syracuse university and how the university requires us to be on a meal plan for 2 years I must suffer through the dining hall food unfortunately. Here is how I rank the dining halls (the only places that take meal swipes)

BRING BACK SCHINE TAKING MEAL SWIPES

  1. Ernie Davis
      2. Make your own omelet station every Moring for breakfast, what a luxury. Other than that Ernie Davis gives me stomach problems. The ambiance is lacking though there is a lot of cement
  2. Graham
      3. Salad bar is elite, you can put grilled chicken on it and they have grilled cheese that you dont need to ask the sandwhich bar to make.
  3. Shaw
      4. Milkshakes and a kosher section. The actual dining hall is very cute it looks like a upscale diner
  4. Sadler
      5. Painfully mid
  5. BBB
      6. I once got a waffle here and it was hard as a rock I could knock on it. Also BBB is not in the bast location.
