All of the great restaurant options around campus not on campus.

I would consider myself a big foodie, meaning I love to eat and enjoy eating. My favorite place around syrcvase that do not involve the dining hall include:

  1. Oh my darling
      Located: 321 S Salina St, Syracuse, NY 13202
    OH MY DARLING WEBSITE

    Oh, my darling has American style cuisine, and the ambiance is to die for. When my parents visit me, I always take them to Oh my darling. My go to is the Rosemary Hot Honey Crispy Chicken and the cookie skillet for dessert. They have everything from a Caesar salad to scallops. The vibes are immaculate, and everything is reasonably priced.

  2. Pastabilities
      3. Located at 311 S Franklin St, Syracuse, NY 13202
    Pastabilities WEBSITE

    Pastabilties, obviously known for their pasta is one of my favorites for a healthy serving of carbs. I love their fettuccini with pink vodka sauce. The line and wait are unbearable but somehow pastabilities makes the wait worth it.

  3. Apizza regionale
      4. Located: 260 W Genesee St, Syracuse, NY 13202
    APIZZA WEBSITE

    Craving a really good oven baked pizza? Apizza regionale is your new go to. Aside for their amazing pizza they have fantastic pasta dishes feature LEONESSA LINGUINE. My favorite pizza is the Ruccilo. Apizza does might have a long wait on occasion, but the snap of the crust makes the experience worth it.

  4. Lemon Grass
      5. Located at 238 W Jefferson St, Syracuse, NY 13202
    LEMON GRASS WEBSITE

    Lemon grass has some fantastic Thai food.

  5. Otro Cinco
      6. located at: 206 S Warren St, Syracuse, NY 13202
    OTRO CINCO WEBSITE

    Otro Cinco is a fantastic blend of Spanish and Mediterranean cuisine. Located inside the historic university building, Otro offers a unique urban dining experience that is both lively and intimate
